Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This classic Italian pasta dish combines a silky cheese sauce with crisp pancetta and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Saute the pancetta and garlic
  3. Beat the eggs and half of the cheese
  4. Cook the pasta
  5. Toss the pasta with the pancetta
  6. Add the beaten egg mixture
  7. Serve!
